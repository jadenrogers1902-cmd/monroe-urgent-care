# Monroe Urgent Care Sitemap

## Purpose

This sitemap defines the page architecture for the new Monroe Urgent Care website. It prioritizes fast patient conversion, local trust, family-focused care, occupational health leads, and clear routing for insurance, self-pay, and emergency-care questions.

## Primary Navigation

- Home
- Urgent Care
- Services
- Occupational Health
- IV Hydration
- Insurance & Self-Pay
- Patient Info
- Contact
- Book Online

## Mobile Sticky Actions

- Call
- Book
- Directions

## Core Pages

### Home

Primary landing page for local patients and families.

Primary goals:
- Help visitors quickly understand that Monroe Urgent Care offers walk-in care in Monroe, OH.
- Route urgent patients to booking, phone, or directions.
- Establish trust through local ownership, affordability, reviews, and service breadth.
- Surface occupational health and IV hydration as secondary paths.

Recommended URL:
- `/`

### Urgent Care

Main urgent-care service page for non-life-threatening illness and injury care.

Primary goals:
- Explain when urgent care is appropriate.
- List major illness, injury, testing, and treatment categories.
- Route users to book, call, or get directions.
- Include emergency-care disclaimer.

Recommended URL:
- `/urgent-care/`

### Services

Overview of all patient-facing and employer-facing services.

Primary goals:
- Provide a scannable service directory.
- Link users to deeper pages.
- Support SEO through service grouping.

Recommended URL:
- `/services/`

### Conditions We Treat

Condition-focused page organized by symptoms and common needs.

Primary goals:
- Help patients confirm Monroe can treat their issue.
- Support local search for common urgent-care needs.
- Link to urgent care, testing, and relevant SEO pages.

Recommended URL:
- `/conditions-we-treat/`

### Testing & Diagnostics

Page for rapid and on-site testing.

Primary goals:
- Present flu, COVID, RSV, strep, pregnancy, urinalysis, TB, and other available testing.
- Clarify which tests are rapid/on-site versus may require outside lab processing if applicable.
- Convert users with symptoms or employer requirements.

Recommended URL:
- `/testing-diagnostics/`

### Physicals

Page for school, sports, camp, work, DOT, and general physicals.

Primary goals:
- Separate physicals from acute illness care.
- Route families, students, workers, and employers to the right next step.

Recommended URL:
- `/physicals/`

### Occupational Health

Employer-focused landing page.

Primary goals:
- Generate employer inquiries.
- Present pre-employment physicals, drug testing, DOT physicals, workers' compensation, fit testing, hearing screening, and other workplace services.
- Position Monroe as a local partner for businesses.

Recommended URL:
- `/occupational-health/`

### Workers' Compensation

Dedicated occupational medicine page for workplace injury care and BWC-related messaging.

Primary goals:
- Clarify support for injured workers and employers.
- Promote BWC-certified provider messaging if confirmed.
- Route employers to contact the clinic.

Recommended URL:
- `/workers-compensation/`

### Drug Testing

Dedicated page for employment and compliance drug screening.

Primary goals:
- Explain drug-screening availability and hours.
- Support employer and individual search traffic.
- Reduce phone confusion around drug-screen hours.

Recommended URL:
- `/drug-testing/`

### IV Hydration

Secondary service page for Monroe IV Drip offerings.

Primary goals:
- Present IV hydration as a wellness service separate from urgent medical care.
- Link to the Monroe IV Drip site if that remains the booking/details destination.
- Avoid distracting from urgent-care conversion on core patient pages.

Recommended URL:
- `/iv-hydration/`

### Insurance & Self-Pay

Affordability and payment reassurance page.

Primary goals:
- Explain accepted insurance at a high level.
- Explain self-pay availability.
- Reduce hesitation for uninsured or cost-sensitive patients.
- Support affordable-care messaging.

Recommended URL:
- `/insurance-self-pay/`

### Patient Info

Pre-visit resource page.

Primary goals:
- Tell patients what to bring.
- Explain walk-ins, appointments, check-in, payment, minors/guardians, forms, and records requests.
- Include urgent care versus ER guidance.

Recommended URL:
- `/patient-info/`

### About

Clinic trust and local identity page.

Primary goals:
- Tell the Monroe Urgent Care story.
- Reinforce locally owned since 2017.
- Present clinic values, staff/facility imagery, and community focus.

Recommended URL:
- `/about/`

### Reviews

Patient testimonial and reputation page.

Primary goals:
- Centralize social proof.
- Feature parent/family, fast-care, affordability, and staff kindness reviews.
- Link to Google reviews if available.

Recommended URL:
- `/reviews/`

### Contact / Location

Location, hours, phone, directions, and contact information page.

Primary goals:
- Give patients the fastest path to visit.
- Show address, map, phone, hours, drug-screen hours, and nearby communities.
- Support local SEO.

Recommended URL:
- `/contact/`

## URL and Redirect Notes

Existing pages should be redirected to the new architecture where applicable:

- Existing urgent care page -> `/urgent-care/`
- Existing employer services page -> `/occupational-health/`
- Existing IV drip page -> `/iv-hydration/`

Confirm final source URLs before launch and set permanent redirects to preserve search value and prevent broken links.

Who it is for

Students, athletes, drivers, employees, and employers.

Physicals help families and workers meet school, sports, camp, job, pre-employment, DOT, or employer documentation requirements.

What to expect

A focused exam based on the form or requirement.

Bring your required paperwork. The visit may include a health history review, basic measurements, exam steps, and form completion when appropriate.

What Monroe provides

Physical options

Family and student physicals

  • School physicals
  • Sports physicals
  • Camp or activity physicals as applicable

Work physicals

  • Pre-employment physicals
  • Work-related physicals
  • Employer-required documentation

DOT physicals

  • Driver physicals
  • Occupational health coordination
  • Related testing as required or available

What to bring

  • Photo ID
  • Required physical form
  • Glasses, contacts, or hearing aids if used
  • Medication list
  • Insurance card or payment method

Insurance and self-pay

Most insurance is accepted for eligible visits, and self-pay is available. Some physicals may be self-pay or employer-paid depending on the requirement.

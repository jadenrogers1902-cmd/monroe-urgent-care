Who it is for

Patients and workers who need testing without a long wait.

Testing can help families, adults, students, and employees understand symptoms, meet workplace requirements, or prepare for the next step in care.

What to expect

Check in, share the reason for testing, and receive next steps.

Some testing may be rapid or on site, while other testing may depend on availability or outside processing. The clinic can clarify timing during your visit.

What Monroe provides

Testing options

Illness testing

  • Flu testing
  • COVID testing
  • RSV testing
  • Strep testing

Everyday diagnostics

  • Pregnancy testing
  • Urinalysis
  • Testing related to UTI symptoms
  • Other testing as available

Workplace testing

  • Drug screens
  • Alcohol screening as available
  • Testing tied to occupational health services

What to bring

  • Photo ID
  • Insurance card, if using insurance
  • Payment method
  • Employer paperwork or testing authorization, if applicable
  • Medication list if relevant

Insurance and self-pay

Most insurance is accepted, and self-pay is available. Some workplace testing may follow employer-specific requirements.

Emergency guidance

If you are experiencing chest pain, trouble breathing, severe bleeding, stroke symptoms, loss of consciousness, major trauma, or another life-threatening emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.

Who it is for

Injured workers, supervisors, HR teams, and safety managers.

Monroe supports evaluation and treatment for work-related injuries that are appropriate for urgent care.

What to expect

Prompt care with employer-aware documentation.

The visit may include injury review, exam, appropriate treatment, follow-up guidance, and paperwork based on employer or claim requirements.

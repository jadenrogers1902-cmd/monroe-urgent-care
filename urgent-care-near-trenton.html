Useful for Trenton families and workers

Monroe Urgent Care helps with common illnesses, sore throats, ear and eye concerns, rashes, minor injuries, school and sports physicals, and workplace medical needs.

Plan your visit

Walk in or book online. Bring a photo ID, insurance card if using insurance, payment method, and any required forms for physicals or employer visits.

Emergency guidance

If you are experiencing chest pain, trouble breathing, severe bleeding, stroke symptoms, loss of consciousness, major trauma, or another life-threatening emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.

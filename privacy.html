Website privacy

Privacy Policy

This page explains how Monroe Urgent Care handles information connected to this website and online appointment tools.

Information you provide

If this website includes contact or employer inquiry forms, Monroe Urgent Care may collect information you choose to submit, such as your name, company, phone number, email address, service interest, and message. Please do not submit sensitive medical information through general website forms.

Appointment scheduling

Online booking links may direct you to a third-party appointment scheduling service. Information submitted through that service is handled according to that service's privacy and security practices.

Website usage

The website may collect basic technical information, such as browser type, pages visited, referring pages, and general device information, to help maintain and improve the website experience.

Third-party services

This website may link to or embed third-party services, including appointment scheduling, Google Maps, Google reviews, and image hosting. Those services may collect information according to their own policies.

How information is used

Information may be used to respond to questions, support appointment or employer service requests, improve the website, and help visitors find Monroe Urgent Care services.

Contact information

For privacy questions about this website, contact Monroe Urgent Care at 513-461-2273 or visit 262 N Main St, Monroe, OH 45050.

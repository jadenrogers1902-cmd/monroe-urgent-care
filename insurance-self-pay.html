Insurance & payment

Insurance & Self-Pay

Monroe Urgent Care provides affordable, same-day care for non-life-threatening illnesses and minor injuries. Many insurance plans are accepted, and self-pay options are available.

Book OnlineCall With Billing QuestionsGet Directions

Most insurance accepted

Bring your insurance card to check-in.

Many insurance plans are accepted. Coverage, copays, deductibles, and visit requirements can vary by plan, so patients are encouraged to call the clinic or their insurer with billing questions.

Self-pay available

Care is available for patients without insurance.

Self-pay options are available. Exact cost depends on the visit type, testing, procedures, supplies, and any additional services needed during the visit.

What affects cost

No surprise promises, just clear factors.

Visit type

Illness care, injury care, physicals, testing, and occupational health visits may be billed differently.

Testing or treatment

Rapid tests, lab work, procedures, medications, or supplies can affect the final amount.

Insurance benefits

Copays, deductibles, coinsurance, network rules, and plan requirements can change patient responsibility.

Emergency guidance

If you are experiencing chest pain, trouble breathing, severe bleeding, stroke symptoms, loss of consciousness, major trauma, or another life-threatening emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.
